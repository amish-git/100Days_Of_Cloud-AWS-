Security Group works at EC2/ENI level.
Stateful-> If inbound allowed, return traffic is automatically allowed.

Common Use: Control access to EC2, Load Balancer, RDS, EKS nodes.
("Who can talk to my EC@ instance?")


NACL(Network ACL):
Subnet-level firewall
Works at VPC subnet level
Stateless --> Need to allow both inbound and outbound explicitly
Rules: Allow + Deny
Evaluated in order(rule number)
Good for blocking specific IPs or enforcing subnet-level boundaries.
